Frequently Asked Questions

Get instant answers to the most common questions about our final year project services. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact our support team for personalized assistance.

How quickly can I get my final year project delivered?

Ready-made projects are delivered instantly after payment confirmation. For custom projects, delivery time depends on complexity: basic projects take 1-2 weeks, advanced projects require 2-4 weeks, and highly complex custom solutions may take 4-6 weeks. We always provide realistic timelines during consultation and keep you updated throughout the development process.

Do you provide complete source code and documentation?

Absolutely! All our projects come with complete, well-commented source code, detailed project documentation in PDF format, implementation guides, database scripts (if applicable), installation instructions, user manuals, and GitHub repository access. You'll have everything needed to understand, implement, and present your project confidently.

Can you help with project presentation and viva preparation?

Yes! We provide comprehensive presentation support including professional PowerPoint templates, speaker notes, technical explanation sessions, mock viva practice, common questions and answers preparation, and one-on-one coaching sessions. Our experts help you understand every aspect of your project to ensure confident presentation and successful viva defense.

What if I need modifications to an existing project?

We offer flexible modification services for both our existing projects and external projects. Whether you need feature additions, technology changes, UI improvements, or functionality enhancements, our team can customize any project according to your specific requirements and university guidelines. Modification costs depend on the complexity of changes required.

Is my project unique and plagiarism-free?

We guarantee 100% original and plagiarism-free content. Each custom project is built from scratch according to your specific requirements. For ready-made projects, we ensure uniqueness by providing different implementations, database designs, and feature variations. All projects undergo thorough plagiarism checks before delivery.

What kind of support do you provide after project delivery?

We provide comprehensive post-delivery support including technical assistance, bug fixes, installation support, project explanation sessions, troubleshooting help, and guidance for project setup. Premium package customers get extended support, while custom project clients receive dedicated support for the duration agreed upon during project initiation.
